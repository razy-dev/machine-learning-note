1. 선형 회귀(Linear Regression)

2. Sample Code

2.1. 샘플 데이터 생성

MAX = 100
x = np.linspace(0, 10, MAX, endpoint=True)
y = 10 * x + np.random.randint(-10, 10, MAX)

2.2. 가설(Hypothesis)

\(h(x) = wx+b\)

# y = w * x + b
def hypothesis(x):
    return w * x + b

2.3. 손실 및 손실 함수(Loss & Cost Function)

Loss

\( \begin{align} loss &= y_i - \hat{y_i} \\ &= y_i - h(x) \\ &= y_i - (wx +b) \\ \end{align} \)

Cost Function

  • 평균 제곱 오차(MSE:Mean Squared Error)

\( \begin{align} cost = MSE(loss) &= \frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}loss^2 \\ &= \frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}(y_i - \hat{y_i})^2 \\ &= \frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}(y_i - (wx +b))^2 \\ \end{align} \)

# cost = 1/n(sum((y - y_pred)^2) = 1/n(sum((y - (w * x + b))^2)
def cost(y: ndarray, x: ndarray):
    return np.sum((y - hypothesis(x)) ** 2) / len(y)

2.4. Optimize

경사하강법(Gradient descent)

선형 최소 제곱(Linear Least Squares) fit

\( \begin{align} cost_{i+1} &= cost_i - \alpha \nabla f(cost_i) \\ &= cost_i - \alpha (\frac{\partial{f}}{\partial{cost}}) \end{align} \)

\(\frac {d}{dw} cost\)

\( \begin{align} \frac {\partial}{\partial{w}}(\frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}(y_i - \hat{y_i})^2) &= \frac {\partial}{\partial{w}}(\frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}(y_i -(wx_i +b))^2) \\ &= \frac {\partial}{\partial{w}}(\frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}(y_i^2 -2y_i(wx_i +b) +(wx_i +b)^2)) \\ &= \frac {\partial}{\partial{w}}(\frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}(y_i^2 -2wx_iy_i -2by_i +w^2x_i^2 +2wbx_i +b^2)) \\ \\ &= \frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}(-2x_iy_i +2wx_i^2 +2bx_i) \\ &= -\frac{2}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}x_i(y_i -wx_i -b) \\ &= -\frac{2}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}x_i(y_i -(wx_i +b)) \\ &= -\frac{2}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}x_i(y_i -\hat{y_i}) \\ \end{align} \)

# dw = -2w/m(sum((y - (w * x +b))) = -2w/m(sum(y-y_pred))
def dcost_dw(y: ndarray, x: ndarray):
    return (-2 / len(y)) * np.sum(x * (y - hypothesis(x)))

\(\frac {d}{db} cost\)

\( \begin{align} \frac {\partial}{\partial{b}}(\frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}(y_i - \hat{y_i})^2) &= \frac {\partial}{\partial{b}}(\frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}(y_i -(wx_i +b))^2) \\ &= \frac {\partial}{\partial{b}}(\frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}(y_i^2 -2y_i(wx_i +b) +(wx_i +b)^2)) \\ &= \frac {\partial}{\partial{b}}(\frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}(y_i^2 -2wx_iy_i -2by_i +w^2x_i^2 +2wbx_i +b^2)) \\ \\ &= \frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}(-2y_i +2wx_i +2b) \\ &= -\frac{2}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}(y_i -wx_i -b) \\ &= -\frac{2}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}(y_i -(wx_i +b)) \\ &= -\frac{2}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}(y_i -\hat{y_i}) \\ \end{align} \)

# db = -2/m(sum((y - (w * x +b))) = = -2/m(sum(y-y_pred))
def dcost_db(y: ndarray, x: ndarray):
    return (-2 / len(y)) * np.sum(y - hypothesis(x))

2.5. Training

w = 5
b = 1
costs = []
lr = 0.001
for e in range(100):
    costs.append(cost(y, x))
    w = w - lr * dcost_dw(y, x)
    b = b - lr * dcost_db(y, x)
